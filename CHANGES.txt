v1.1.0, 2014/07/07 -- New functionality added: substituting '_' and '.'.
- Iterating on the list of nicks to find '.' or "_" and replace them according to:
	if '.' in n: 
		adding n.replace('.', '_')
		adding n.replace('.', '')
	if '_' in n: 
		adding n.replace('_', '.')
		adding n.replace('_', '')
This will help with identifying new accounts.
